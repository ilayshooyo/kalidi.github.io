About Me

Advancing Public Health through Evidence-Based Research.

I am a PhD Candidate in Public Health at Lund University, with a research affiliation at Dalarna University. My work focuses on Social Epidemiology and Child Health, bridging the gap between clinical nutrition and social determinants of health.

My current research investigates the prevalence and determinants of malnutrition among under-five children, both in Swedish contexts and global humanitarian settings. I have a specific interest in evaluating the efficacy of interventions such as Unconditional Cash Transfers (UCT) combined with nutrition counseling.
